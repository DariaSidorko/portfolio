Shop-Easy

About the Project

Shop-Easy application was created during the 2015 Sears Hackathon.
Target Area: Mobile and Social Commerce; Post Purchase Support.

The Shop-Easy application was designed by a team of three people and was designed to target an in-store customer shopping experience. The purpose of the application is to provide better customer support and improve the quality of the shopping process.

  • The Shop-Easy application allows customers to do an express check out on their mobile device by scanning barcode of each item with their phone as they shop, instead of waiting in line at the register.
  • This application allows the customer to keep track of the number of items in their cart, the price of each item, as well as a total price of the purchase.
  • At the mobile express checkout all bonus points and coupons can be applied with just a few clicks.
  • By scanning an item’s barcode with the application the customer can pull up a full description of the item and its rating.
  • In-store customer attendant can be requested with few easy steps through this application.

Team: Daria Sidorko, Jiyoon Seo, Desislava Yovcheva

Proccess

The design process included an analysis of the current shopping experience at Sears stores and research of how mobile devices shaped customer’s shopping process. During this project we focused on understanding how a mobile device can improve the shopping process as well as how the customer and store can benefit. We researched the current mobile application market as well as latest web and mobile trends to develop a competitive product.

Market Research

Market research showed that mobile devices affected customer’s shopping experience. A large number of shoppers use their mobile device in store for such things as: product reviews; price comparison; redemption of in-store discounts and etc. Today, customers have to use multiple applications to do all of those manipulations. Besides that, consumers not only want, but expect a personalized customer service experience. As a result of this research, our team designed an application, which handles those functions and adds new functionalities to modernize the customer shopping experience.

Brainstorming

After analyzing customer needs, we brainstormed all the features application can offer. Our team identified that there are several most common functions people use throughout their shopping experience.

*Initially one of the ideas was to provide cartless shopping experience, where customers would use the phone to add items in the digital cart and pick up a ready to go, stocked by store associates, cart at the exit. Upon further research, we indicated to many requirements and arrangements that stores would have to do to make this feature possible, and decided to exclude this from our final product.

    Wireframing

    Final Project

    Shopping cart is the home screen of the application. To start shopping, press “add your first item” which will transition you to the next screen - a regular cell phone barcode scanner. Any desired item can be added into the digital cart by simply scanning the barcode of the item.

    After adding the first item into the cart, a full description of the item will be provided. The barcode scanner function can be used to add items to the cart or to pull up full descriptions of any item. The rating and reviews of each item is also available together with the full item description. Within only one click this item can be added to the digital cart for simple check out.

    The digital cart allows the user to track all the items in their cart along with the prices for each individual item as well as the total price of the purchase. The user can add more items to their cart a needed by pressing the “Add new item” button.

    Sears Help Center

    The mobile Sears help center is designed to help store representatives be more efficient with their time. Many issues can be solved much faster over the phone even if the customer is in the store. The option to request customer assistance within a few clicks on the phone is easy and convenient. Also, personalized accounts of the representative and customer will help the Sears representative to locate a customer in the store, as well as provide superior customer service.

    To request Sears representative assistance, press “sears help” button and choose between “In Person Assistance” and “Call Sears Representative”.

    After pressing “Invite in Person Representative” indicate your location in the store by providing isle number.

    Shortly after, you get connected with one of the Sears representatives.

    By pressing and holding icon of the representative in the chat, you can look at his profile picture, which will help you to recognize him.

BizScan

About the Project

BizScan application was created during the capstone class at Lake Washington Institute of Technology.

The application was designed and developed by a team of three people. This application allows the user to scan a business card and then seamlessly transfer the information into the native phone book of the phone within three simple steps.

Goal: analyze available applications and design a simple, clean and single task oriented application.

Team: Daria Sidorko, Jiyoon Seo, Desislava Yovcheva<

My contribution:
Market Research
Brainstorming
Sketching
Prototyping

Proccess

Market Research

After researching the market for applications to scan business cards, we found out that there are many similar applications available. All of these applications have very confusing UI as well as additional unnecessary functions which confuse people.

  • Many applications require the user to sign up and provide personal information such as an email address.
  • Some application offer the user to exchange business card with other people through the app or create a group chat with other people.
  • Other applications offer the user to make a business card, providing outdated templates.
  • Number of those applications create separate contact book to store the information.

Brainstorming

Our team was focused on providing minimal functionality in this application. The intended purpose of the application is to scan a business card and transfer the information in the business card to the phone. The design of the application had to be so easy, that the user would not have any question on how to use it.

Sketching

First Simple Prototype

Final Project

The home screen was designed to provide maximum comfort for the user. Two large squared buttons are placed in such a way that makes them accessible by using only one hand. At the same time the two buttons provide most of the functionality of the app. Two different bright colors were chosen to associate each function of the application not only with the icon, but also with the color to make it easier to use in the future.

This application provides the following three options: option one is to save the captured image of a business card into the phones default image library. Option two would be to set the captured image as the contact image of the contact it would be creating. Lastly there would be an option to check the phonebook for duplicates before adding a contact.

The process of scanning the business card takes only three easy steps: choose, scan, save.
